About Me

Here is a little bit about myself, my interests, background, and goals.

Background

I was born in Ramotswa and grew up in Gaborone. I had a great upbringing, went to school, finished high school, and now I am studying Business Intelligence and Data Analytics at BAC. Outside school, I am the 2025 Director of Programs and Training for JCI Gaborone, a Non-Profit Organization centered around promoting societal peace and empowerment through youthful leadership and free enterprise. In terms of experience, I’ve gained enough to get me where I am. That is all.

Interests

I love music, stories, games, books, and movies—anything that sparks imagination. My passion is to contribute to the world’s growth and innovation, either with my talents or skills. I once hoped to be responsible for something that would be mentioned in the same breath as the Mona Lisa.

Goals

My primary goal right now is to graduate and be happy, and that goes hand in hand with many things. In a nutshell.
